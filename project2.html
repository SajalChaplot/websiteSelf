Techniche.org

Techniche is the tech festival of IIT Guwahati. The website is the digital base which supports event launch, branding and marketing. The previous website design lacked the flexibilty of mobile responsiveness and was slow to be opened on mobile data, leaving many college students frustrated. I took charge as Head of Web operations, designed and developed a mobile first solution with tools for internal team to better manage participation.
